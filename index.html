What is The Foreign Exchange Market & How Can I Profit?

The Foreign Exchange Market, also known as Forex, FX or currency trading, is a decentralized global market where all the currencies in the world are traded.

Did You Know?

The Forex market is the most liquid market in the world with an average daily trading volume exceeding $6 trillion USD.

This means more trading volume is transacted in the forex markets in a single day than most countries individually transact over a year.
